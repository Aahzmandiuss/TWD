Deck Name: Tragic, fast and beautiful (2005)
Description:
A variation of the Cel-Gun theme: Relatively strong forward power and combining gun combat with Majesty untap. So the signature move was to shoot, use Majesty as an additional strike in order to untap, ideally taste, then Psyche! to finish the victim off. Good times.
