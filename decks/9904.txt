Deck Name: Saulot & Capuchin Team
Description: Based on M. Schumacher "Hard Boiled" deck. In my opinion it is very strong toolbox-wall deck. If you are patient you should go forward with 3 minions. At the beginning it is important to bring first Saulot and then Cappuchin. Nine cards on your hand give you big advantage. I will probably change/add sniper rifle and one more delaying tactics or Direct Intervention.
